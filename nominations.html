Why have the roles changed?

We’ve spent the past few months working on a new structure for the union and listening to your feedback. The new officer roles and inclusion reps are one part of these changes.

Find out more about the changes

What’s the photo for?

We need a photograph of you to use while promoting the elections.

What might make me good for a role?

Think about the experience and skills you have that would make you successful in the role. You might have experience running events, organising a team, or representing students at course committee. Keep it to 150 words.

What should my goals be?

Not just ‘represent all students and hold the school to account’ – that’s already part of the job. What changes do you want to see at Central and think you could get done in a year?
