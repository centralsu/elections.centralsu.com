Guidance

Nominations will close on Monday 25 October at 5pm.
What is a co-candidate?

If two candidates are running as a pair, they should be entered seperately on one form. If a candidate is running alone, the co-candidate section can be left blank.

What is a manifesto?

The manifesto describes your aims for the year. Download the template, fill in your details and upload it to this form as either a PDF or word document.

What should the photo be?

We need a photograph of the candidate (or candidates, if standing as a pair) to use while marketing the by-election.
