Events officer candidates

Find out more about this role

Caolan McGinley

First year student on BA drama, applied theatre and education. Have a question? Contact Caolan via email.

Aims for the year
  • Create a trans-specific group for trans people to meet and share advice and support
  • Run education sessions on how to use respectful, trans-inclusive language
  • Organise trans-specific open mic/comedy nights

Trans people often feel, or are made to feel, that because they are a minority, they have no voice. I want to challenge this and create a community at Central where we feel supported in raising any problems we might encounter as trans students. I would like to create a group for trans and non-binary people to meet and get to know each other. I am to provide training for both staff and students on how to use inclusive language, how to be respectful of other people’s pronouns and what to do if you or someone else gets them wrong. I will be as informative as possible for anyone trans or cis, who has questions or is seeking advice. I will also work closely with the LGBTQ+ officer to provide the best support possible for trans students, and liaise with other trans student groups to find ways to improve things for the wider trans community. I also want to remind people that being trans does not always have to be a struggle, and provide a safe space for trans people to share their job and creativity.

Nominated by Ellie Bibby
