Election schedule

See what’s happening when and where as the elections progresses.

Tuesday 14 June

The elections are announced.

Nominations open and can be made via this website.

Monday 20 June

Nominations close at 12pm midday.

Information session for all candidates at 6pm.

Candidates are announced at 9pm with profiles on this website.

Campaigning begins at 9pm.

Thursday 23 June

Campaigning ends at 5pm.

Voting opens at 5pm. Information will be emailed to each member.

Friday 24 June

Voting closes at 5pm, after 24 hours open.

Results announced at 7pm.

Friday 1 August

Elected officers begin term of office.

The real work begins.
